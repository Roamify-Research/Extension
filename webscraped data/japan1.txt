Japan is famous for its rich cultural heritage, 
including traditional arts like tea ceremonies, ikebana (flower

'sakura' season, is celebrated with hanami (flower viewing) gatherings 
and festivals across the country as people enjoy the fleeting beauty 
of the cherry blossoms.
